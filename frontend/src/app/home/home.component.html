Our story

We believe in good food. We launched Fresh Pan Pizza Best Excuse Awards on our Facebook fan page. Fans were given situations where they had to come up with witty and fun excuses. The person with the best excuse won the Best Excuse Badge and won Pizzeria vouchers. Their enthusiastic response proved that Pizzeria's Fresh Pan Pizza is the Tastiest Pan Pizza Ever!

Ever since we launched the Tastiest Pan Pizza, everyone has been able to resist the softest, cheesiest, crunchiest, butteriest Domino's Fresh Pan Pizza. They have been eating the stage in the middle of a performance and even finding excuses to be disqualified in a football match.

Ingredients
